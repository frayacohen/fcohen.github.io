About me

I have a passion for software and hardware development, and I've been engaged in chip development for over 20 years. I'm a Verification Manager at Cirrus Logic and actively involved in tools and methodologies across the product development cycle. Before joining Cirrus I worked as a Verification Consultant with Synopsys for over 6 years working directly with client companies on-site and architecting solutions for their regression management flows and coverage closure utilities.
